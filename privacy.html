Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Local-First Data

Audipella is designed as a local-first application. Your audio recordings, track data, and project files (.adp) are stored exclusively on your device's local file system. We do not upload your audio content to any external servers.

Permissions Usage

  • Microphone: Used solely to capture audio for your projects. Recording only occurs when you explicitly tap the record button.
  • File System: Required to save your projects, generate waveforms, and export mixed audio stems to your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or audio data to outside parties. Your creative work remains yours and stays on your hardware.
