Help & Support

🎙️ How do I align recorded takes? +
In the timeline editor, press and hold any take, then drag it to the desired start point. The snap guide helps you align to beats or markers.
📁 What is an .adp project bundle? +
.adp bundles contain all audio files, waveforms, project metadata, markers, and effects. Use import/export to share full projects with other Audipella users.
🔊 Can I extract audio from video? +
Yes. Import video from your library — Audipella extracts audio and lets you trim before adding to the timeline.
⚙️ How to adjust drag sensitivity & workspace duration? +
Go to Settings → Workspace: you can adjust drag sensitivity, default duration, sync options, and cache cleanup.
🎚️ What is Pro Vocal feature? +
Pro Vocal applies intelligent noise reduction tailored for vocals, reducing background hiss while preserving clarity.
📱 Does Audipella need internet? +
No backend or server — works completely offline after install.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026


Audipella respects your privacy. The app operates 100% offline — no cloud servers, no telemetry, no data collection. All recordings, projects, and exported files remain on your device.


  • What we DO NOT collect: personal data, audio content, usage analytics, or location. There is no account system, no sign-up, no tracking.
  • Local storage: Audipella stores your projects, audio files, and waveform caches inside the app's sandboxed storage. You can delete them at any time via the app's settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Third-party access: None. Audipella does not integrate any analytics or crash reporters. We don't share anything because we don’t have anything to share.
  • Permissions Usage:
    • Microphone: Used solely to capture audio for your projects. Recording only occurs when you explicitly tap the record button.
    • File System: Required to save your projects, generate waveforms, and export mixed audio stems to your device.
  • Children’s privacy: The app does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13.
  • Contact: If you have questions, contact us at privacy@audipella.com — but rest assured, we built this to be a private, offline creative tool.
